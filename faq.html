Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I donate food to food shelters?

To donate food to food shelters, follow these steps:

  1. Visit our website and log in or create an account.
  2. Click on the "Donate Food" button.
  3. Enter details about the food items you want to donate, including type, quantity, and expiry date.
  4. Provide your location for food pickup or delivery.
  5. Confirm the donation.

Our team will arrange to collect the food from your location and distribute it to the nearest food shelter.

2. How can I request food assistance?

Requesting food assistance is simple:

  1. Log in to your account on our website.
  2. Browse the available food items in the inventory.
  3. Select the food items you need and add them to your cart.
  4. Confirm your order and provide your location for delivery.
  5. Our team will deliver the food to the nearest food shelter, and you can collect it from there.

This service is available to individuals and families in need.

3. How do I find the nearest food shelter?

Locating the nearest food shelter is easy:

  • When you place a food order, our system will automatically identify the nearest food shelter to your location.
  • Directions to the shelter will be provided, making it convenient for you to collect your food assistance.
You can also check the "Find Food Shelters" section on our website for a list of nearby shelters.

4. Is there a cost for receiving food assistance?

No, there is no cost for receiving food assistance through our platform. Our mission is to reduce food waste and help those in need, so all food assistance is provided free of charge.
